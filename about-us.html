The Company

Originally in 1996, Catco Aerospace FZE focused on the Aviation and Defence sectors supplying OEM spare parts and equipment to major domestic airlines and military establishments in UAE and Oman. We have since expanded to include the wholesale distribution of top-quality brands like 3M, Honeywell, Anderol, Apex Tool Group Companies, Polyken, and Solvay. We are proud to hold Authorised Distributor status with these great brands. Our success is built on the relationships that we create and nurture for it is only by listening to our customers that we learn more and only by being realistic and honest do we win their trust. Catco Aerospace does not use a fixed sales system. We build bespoke solutions for every company that we supply. As every company is different to another, no fixed sales system will fit every company. Our core strengths are found in our ability to always deliver orders on time and in line with customer expectations.

Catco Aerospace FZE emerged in the Gulf Region Aviation Market in the year 1996 and has continuously grown since then, building a reputation for products that are of premium quality, always available, and cost-efficient with a dependable after sale support. Having developed a thorough understanding the market and each client’s unique requirements over the years of its nonstop operation, it has become one of the most established and leading distributors of Spare parts and Consumables, Specialty Lubricants, Hand and Power tools, and Paints, Coatings, and Cleaners in the Middle Eastern region. Its untainted credibility has also earned its way to partnerships with many major aviation companies giving Catco Aerospace the opportunity to be a reliable support and of valuable contribution to the aviation industry.

What We Do

We offer a wide range of Spare parts and Consumables, Specialty Lubricants, Hand and Power tools, and Paints, Coatings, and Cleaners making us the preferred provider in the market. We hold an official distributorship status of the brands that we sell which ensures that our stocks are all original. On top of all these, we have exemptional customer support which provides dependable assistance for every query and concern at hand.
